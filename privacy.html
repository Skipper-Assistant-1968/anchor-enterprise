Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2026

This page covers the website and pre-engagement communications. Confidential information shared during a paid engagement is governed by a separate mutual non-disclosure agreement, signed before work begins.

What We Collect

When you contact us: name, email, phone, professional details, and the content of your messages.

When you visit the site: standard web analytics — browser type, IP address, pages viewed, and referring source.

When you become a client: billing details, handled through our payment processor.

How We Use and Share It

We use information to respond to inquiries, schedule and manage engagements, send opted-in text messages, process payments, maintain the site, and meet legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers (SMS, payments, hosting) who are contractually required to protect it, or when legally required.

SMS / Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number and opt in, you consent to receive SMS / MMS messages from Anchor Enterprise.

  • Types: scheduling, confirmations, and engagement-related communications.
  • Frequency: typically not exceeding 10 messages per month.
  • Rates: message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out: reply STOP to any message.
  • Help: reply HELP, or contact us below.
  • Consent to text messages is not required to engage Anchor Enterprise.

Anchor Enterprise complies with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and applicable federal and state regulations.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us. We use reasonable safeguards to protect data and retain it only as long as needed.
